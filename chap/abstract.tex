\begin{cabstract}
随着人脸检测与识别算法研究的深入和视频监控需求的增长，面向监控视频场景下的人脸识别技术逐渐成为一个新的研究热点。智能化是视频监控的必然趋势，基于视频监控的人脸识别技术是融合了模式识别、图像处理、计算机视觉等多学科的具有挑战性的课题。由于它提供了一种可靠、方便的身份鉴别途径，而且人脸包含了重要的生物信息，具有极高的利用价值，因此有着广泛的应用环境和广阔的应用前景。然而，在监控视频当中，场景复杂多变、光照不受控制、图像质量低，给人脸检测和识别带来了很大的挑战。对于监控环境下的人脸检测和识别问题，研究者们开展了深入的研究，本文在大量研究者工作的基础上，主要考虑监控场景下多姿态人脸的检测与识别问题。

针对监控环境下人脸形变、尺度、摄像头角度等问题，本课题采用了一种基于可变型部件模型（DPM）的人脸检测方法。实验结果证明，该方法不但提高了对多人脸各个姿态的检测率，而且降低了人脸检测的误检率。并经过多线程和指令集优化后，在i5 CPU的PC上，对720p监控视频取得每秒25帧的速率。

针对人脸检测后的归一化问题，需要对多姿态的人脸进行特征点检测。针对特征点问题当中的形变、遮挡及实时性的要求，本课题提出了一种基于两级级联回归的人脸特征点定位方法。该方法能够对多姿态的人脸实现精准的定位，而且保证在算法快速收敛，实现定位的实时性。

针对监控环境下的人脸尺度、光照、形变复杂多变等特点，本课题提出了一种基于两级多任务度量学习的人脸识别方法。传统的度量学习方法在应用于人脸识别时会产生过拟合、对图像噪声缺乏稳健性和训练速度慢等问题，且只针对从单一图像中一个特征学习马氏距离的度量，无法表达人脸图像的有效信息。初次之外，很多度量学习方法忽视了多分类问题当中共享的类间相似性。人脸辨识问题可以被看作一个多分类问题，且不同人的人脸有很强的视觉相似性。基于此，本章提出了两级多任务度量学习方法用于人脸辨识，通过等价约束的的方式学习一个度量矩阵。在第一级当中，采用了多种特征。在第二级当中，将每个人当作一个特定的子任务，而每个任务的标签信息代表着身份信息，最终通过多任务投票机制进行分类。该方法充分利用了成对的标签信息，并将该信息迁移到多分类任务当中。实验结果表明，该方法比传统多任务学习方法和多任务支持向量机有着更好的效果。

\end{cabstract}

\begin{eabstract}
With the development of face detection and recognition technology, face recognition in video surveillance scenes has become a hot research topic for the growing demand of surveillance. As intelligent video surveillance is an inevitable trend, face recognition in video surveillance scenes is a challenging task involving pattern recognition, image processing, computer vision and other subjects. Besides, it provides a reliable and convenient identity verification way and therefore has a wide range of application with broad prospects. However, the task becomes more difficult for the complex scenes, uncontrolled illumination and low resolution in video surveillance. Deep studies for face detection and face recognition in video surveillance scenes have been conducted, based on which the problem of face recognition in video surveillance is focused. In this project, we mainly consider the pose-varied face detection and recognition problems.

In order to solve the problems such as human face pose, scale, camera angle and so on in video surveillance. This paper adopts Deformable part model (DPM) for face detection. The experimental results show that the proposed method not only improves the accuracy of the multiple faces, but also reduces the false positive rate of face detection. And after the multi thread and instruction set optimization, the speed of face detection reaches at 25 frames per second for 720p video with an i5 CPU.

A novel face recognition method is proposed in our work to solve the problems of different face scales, illuminations and complex deformations in video surveillance scenes. As over-fitting problems occurs when applying traditional metric learning methods which also train slowly and are not robust to image noises. Therefore, to learn a single metric matrix is not a favorable way for the task of face recognition in video surveillance scenes as discriminative information cannot be captured. Besides, Many metric learning approaches neglect that the real world multi-class problems share strong visual similarities, which can be exploited by learning discriminative models. As we know, the face identification problem can be regarded a multi classification problem and faces with different share strong visual similarity. Based on this, a Two-level Multi-task Metric Learning (TMTL) method is presented to learn a distance measure from equivalence constraints. Multiple features are adopted to represent the image information and learn the distance matrices in the first level. Then the task-specific learning paradigm and multi-task voting mechanism make full use of pairwise equivalence labels, which induces knowledge from anonymous pairs to multi-classification. The experiments results demonstrate that our method outperforms the recent multi-task learning approaches and multi-class support vector machine.
\end{eabstract}
